# Accessibility Nutrition Label Audit

## Problem/Feature Description

The release team is preparing an iOS accessibility evidence audit for App Store Accessibility Nutrition Labels in a media-heavy iOS 26 app. This is not a general App Review policy review. The app includes account setup, browsing, search, playback, downloads, captions/audio-track selection, and subscription management. The current draft claim list came from an older internal checklist and includes VoiceOver, Switch Control, Full Keyboard Access, Closed Captions / SDH, Audio Descriptions, Larger Text, Reduce Motion, and Increase Contrast.

Before anyone enters answers in App Store Connect, the team needs a corrected audit of which product-page accessibility claims are current and what evidence to collect.

## Output Specification

Write `nutrition-label-audit.md`. Include the corrected claim list, which proposed claims should be renamed or moved out of App Store Connect answers, the evidence the team should gather before answering, and an explicit warning that App Store accessibility answers must stay accurate and must not be treated as marketing claims.
