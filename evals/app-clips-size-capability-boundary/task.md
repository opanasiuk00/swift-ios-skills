A product manager wants a venue check-in App Clip. The proposed feature list includes:

- QR and NFC entry at the venue.
- A demo link for a sales team.
- Live Activities while the visitor is checked in.
- CloudKit for venue data.
- App Intents so Siri can start check-in.
- In-app purchases for premium access.
- BackgroundTasks for cleanup after checkout.
- Large bundled videos and other media.
- Ad attribution, tracking permission prompts, and a custom URL scheme for partner deep links.

Write a concise feasibility review. Say which pieces can belong in the App Clip, which should move to the full app or a sibling Apple-domain implementation, and which size and invocation constraints should drive the architecture.
